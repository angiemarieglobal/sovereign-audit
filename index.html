The Sovereign Audit

A 10-minute mirror that shows you where you are still waiting outside your own authority.

Before you begin

You have done the work. You decided, you widened, you let yourself be held. And still, in the rooms that matter, you wait. You glance at the room to see whether you are allowed to lead. You price yourself by what others will accept. You dim your voice to keep your place.

Whose permission are you still waiting for to be who you already are.

You have called it humility. Reading the room. It is none of those. It is a throne you keep standing beside, waiting for someone to tell you that you are allowed to sit in it. This is not a test. It is a mirror.

The 90 second throne

Sit up. Both feet on the floor. Crown of your head lifting. Bring to mind the room where you wait, the place you keep glancing for the nod. And just for ninety seconds, stop waiting. Take the seat. Simply. The way someone sits in a chair that has always been theirs.

I am the one who chooses.
I do not wait to be chosen.

Feel the part of you that wants to look around first, to check whether you are allowed. That glance is the waiting. Let your spine lengthen by one inch. The throne was always yours. You were just waiting for permission to sit down.

The doorway

Most people will read this, feel the recognition, and go back to waiting for the nod by morning. You do not have to be most people.

Sovereign is twenty-two days where you stop being held and become the one who holds. You build your own field, your own offer, your own voice, until you set the frequency the room calibrates to. You have been the seeker long enough. This is where you become the source.
